Rwandan Proverbs

Wisdom of a Thousand Hills presents a curated collection of traditional Rwandan sayings. This project digitizes and shares proverbs that encapsulate centuries of cultural insight. Each proverb offers a glimpse into Rwandan thought and values, often revealing universal truths. By exploring these sayings, users can gain new perspectives on life, relationships, and society. The project aims to preserve this oral tradition and make it accessible to a global audience, bridging cultural gaps and fostering understanding.
